Introduction

Have you ever thought of creating a colour out of your name and didn't know how to do it? Look no further, it is possible now!

Colour your name is a small project created to generate a colour out of your name. When a user enters his/her name (first, middle and last) in the fields below and submit, the code takes name entered from the input field and do a math and generates colour out of your name.
